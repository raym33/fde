# Shopify ecommerce skill

When the client runs Shopify or a similar ecommerce workflow:

- prioritize support automation, product Q&A, returns handling, and order status requests,
- use storefront, order, and policy data as primary retrieval sources,
- define escalation for refunds, fraud indicators, and policy exceptions,
- keep customer-visible responses aligned with store policy and shipping rules,
- and include measurable KPIs such as first-response time, deflection rate, refund error rate, and conversion impact.
